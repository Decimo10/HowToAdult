V E G E T A R I A N

Look, we didn’t lump you together with the vegans! We’re not dumb!

As a vegetarian, it’s sometimes hard to get enough protein so we’re going to provide you with some meat substitutes

  • Beans are magical. They’re cheap--perfect for your perpetually empty wallet--healthy, and filling. Not only that, but you can use them in a variety of meals from soups to salads to even burgers.
  • Cauliflower. You’ve probably heard of this one before. If not, well cauliflower can be used to substitute for chicken, steak, and gluten! Check out how to cook them just right: https://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/08/19/paleo-recipes-with-cauliflower-delish_n_5689172.html

V E G A N

As a vegan, it’s also hard to get your daily dose of protein. But don’t worry, nuts have you covered! Nuts are pretty filling, so they make good meat substitutes. Use them to make a burger or even cheese substitutes.

Potatoes can be used to make desserts. Yep, you read that right. Read all about it here: http://www.onegreenplanet.org/vegan-food/recipe-chocolate-potato-cake/

C O O K I N G   H A C K S

  • Cutting onions that are chilled won’t cause you to tear up as much
  • Brine (salt water with herbs and spices) your meat before cooking it to ensure it doesn’t dry out
  • Use pineapple juice to tenderize your meat! For those science nerds out there, it contains the enzyme bromelain, which acts as a natural tenderizer. Make sure it’s fresh pineapple juice, because the canned version doesn’t contain bromelain
  • Don’t throw away your chicken scraps--you can use the bones to create broth that can be used in soup or something else
  • Double bag your bread before you put it in your freezer so it’ll last longer
  • If your cheese is moldy, cut off the mold and the rest will be perfectly safe to eat
  • You can freeze your vegetables for up to a year after being cut (depends on the vegetable)
  • Leave an open container of baking soda in the fridge to remove odors
  • Keep the avocado pit in your guacamole to keep it fresh and prevent it from turning brown
